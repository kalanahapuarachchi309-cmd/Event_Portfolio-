Case Study

JOCKEY Summit | Star Garments Group

Proposed Interior, Installations, and Event Planning

Katunayake BOI Zone, Sri Lanka

Symphony Events LK produced a fast-tracked, full-scale experience for the three-day JOCKEY Summit, translating Star Garments mood imagery into a fully realized interior and event environment within days.

Brand JOCKEY
Event Window Oct 6 - 8
Scope Event & Interior Ops

The engagement covered concept development, approvals, production planning, procurement, on-site installations, and logistics oversight so that Star Garments could host talent, partners, and guests inside a purpose-built summit arena.

Scope and Deliverables

  • Master visual concept for the full summit journey.
  • Graphic systems built through illustrations, SketchUp models, and digital drawings.
  • End-to-end interior design package with 3D visuals, 3D models, and detailed layout planning.
  • Complete interior detail drawings, fit-out documentation, and lighting layouts.
  • Event management, installations, planning, and logistics delivered by Symphony Events LK.

Operations Focus

  • Delivered full interiors aligned with Star Garments mood images.
  • Developed equipment visuals, lighting concepts, and content assets.
  • Designed furniture, fit-outs, and accessory groupings by zone.
  • Curated light fittings, indoor equipment, and active-wear arena bicycles.
  • Selected color palettes, frames, prints, and bespoke furnishings that reinforced the brand story.

Result: A distinct, high-energy summit experience built under a compressed timeline without compromising detail.
